About Us

As the mission is being run by a charitable trust namely Rahmat-E-Alam Educational & Welfare Trust, Rahmat-E-Alam Mission does charitable works for the Muslim community. It helps underprivileged Muslims students with its own sponsorship programme and has scholarship programs to help other communities’ needy students. The Mission also provides G.D. Scholarships to the needy and meritorious students with the benevolent support from G.D. Charitable Society (a philanthropic organization which is being run under the patronage of the great benefactor of West Bengal Alhaz Mustak Hossain).

Mission

To convey knowledge, skills and positive attitude to the underprivileged students. To hammer the spirit of compassion towards younger and respect towards the elder. To motivate backward Muslim students with moral and ethical values. To promote the values of right citizenship and selfless service to the humanity.

Vision

To provide high quality cost effective modern education with Islamic values to the students belonging to the socially, educationally and economically backward Muslim community.
