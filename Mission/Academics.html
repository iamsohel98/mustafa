ADMISSION

Admission Alert: Parents are requested to contact in Office.

The admission process is designed to be efficient and transparent. The school accepts students from all backgrounds and aims to provide a conducive learning environment.

Parents are required to submit a completed application form along with the necessary documents.

Interviews and entrance tests may be conducted based on the class of admission.

The school follows an inclusive admission policy, ensuring no discrimination on the basis of race, religion, or socioeconomic status.
