Admission Test 2025

The Admission Test 2025 for Classes V - IX will be held in March 2025. All interested students are required to register by February 2025. Further details will be provided in due course.

The schedule for the admission test is as follows:

  • March 1, 2025 - English
  • March 2, 2025 - Mathematics
  • March 3, 2025 - Science

For any queries regarding the admission test, please contact the school administration office at (555) 123-4567 or email at admissions@schoolname.com.
